1. Verb Tenses

Present Simple: Used for habits and general truths.

  • Example: She plays the piano every day.

Past Simple: Used for completed actions in the past.

  • Example: He visited the museum last week.

Future Simple: Used for future actions or decisions.

  • Example: I will call you tomorrow.

2. Sentence Structure

A basic sentence in English follows the Subject-Verb-Object (SVO) structure:

  • Example: The cat (Subject) drinks (Verb) milk (Object).

3. Articles

Articles (a, an, the) are used before nouns:

  • A: Used before words starting with a consonant sound.
  • An: Used before words starting with a vowel sound.
  • The: Used for specific nouns.

Example: She found a book and an apple on the table.
